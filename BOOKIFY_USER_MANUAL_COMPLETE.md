## Part 1: Getting Started

### Creating an Account

**Step 1:** Go to the Bookify homepage

**Step 2:** Click "Sign Up" or "Create Account"

**Step 3:** Choose your sign-up method:
- **Email/Password:** Fill in:
  - First Name
  - Last Name
  - Email Address
  - Password (must be secure)
  - Confirm Password
- **Google Sign-Up:** Click "Continue with Google" and select your account

**Step 4:** Accept Terms of Service and Privacy Policy

**Step 5:** Click "Sign Up"

**üéâ Bonus:** You'll receive 150 welcome points when you sign up!

---

### Logging In

**Method 1: Email/Password**
1. Enter your email address
2. Enter your password
3. Click "Log In"

**Method 2: Google**
1. Click "Continue with Google"
2. Select your Google account

**Forgot Password?**
1. Click "Forgot Password?"
2. Enter your email
3. Check your email for reset instructions

---

### First Steps

**For Guests:**
1. ‚úÖ Verify your email (check your inbox)
2. ‚úÖ Complete your profile (optional but recommended)
3. ‚úÖ Start exploring listings!

**For Future Hosts:**
1. ‚úÖ Verify your email
2. ‚úÖ Complete guest profile first
3. ‚úÖ Then proceed to Host Setup (see Host Guide)

## Part 2: Guest Guide

### Guest Dashboard

**Access:** Click "Dashboard" in the navigation menu

**What You'll See:**
- **Welcome message** with your name
- **Statistics:**
  - Total bookings
  - Nights booked
  - Saved places (favorites)
  - Reviews given
- **Recent bookings** (last 5)
- **Monthly booking trends**
- **Total points balance**
- **Total spent this year**

**Quick Actions:**
- View all bookings
- Explore listings
- Check wallet balance
- View points

---

### Exploring Listings

**Access:** Click "Explore" in the navigation menu

**Step 1: Choose Category**
- **Homes** - Accommodations (apartments, houses, etc.)
- **Experiences** - Activities and tours
- **Services** - Professional services

**Step 2: Search & Filter**
- **Search by location:** Enter city, region, or address
- **Select dates:** Choose check-in and check-out (for Homes)
- **Number of guests:** Adults, children, infants
- **Price range:** Set minimum and maximum price
- **Filters:** Amenities, property type, etc.

**Step 3: Browse Results**
- Click on any listing card to see details
- Use arrows to scroll through photos
- Read descriptions, amenities, and reviews
- Check pricing and availability

**Step 4: Save to Favorites**
- Click the heart icon (‚ù§Ô∏è) on any listing
- View saved listings in "Favorites" section

---

### Making a Booking

**Step 1: Select a Listing**
- Click on a listing card from Explore or Favorites
- Review all details carefully

**Step 2: Choose Dates & Guests**
- **For Homes:** Select check-in and check-out dates
- **For Experiences/Services:** Select date and time slot
- Enter number of guests (adults, children, infants)

**Step 3: Review Pricing**
- Base price
- Cleaning fee (if applicable)
- Service fee (20%)
- **Discounts applied:**
  - Promotional offers (auto-applied)
  - Coupon codes (enter manually)
  - Rewards (select from your rewards)
- **Total amount**

**Step 4: Apply Discounts (Optional)**
- **Coupon Code:** Enter code in the coupon field
- **Rewards:** Click "Apply Reward" and select from available rewards
- Discounts stack: Promo ‚Üí Coupon ‚Üí Reward

**Step 5: Complete Payment**
- **Option 1: PayPal**
  - Click "Pay with PayPal"
  - Complete PayPal checkout
- **Option 2: E-Wallet**
  - Ensure sufficient balance
  - Click "Pay with Wallet"
  - Confirm payment

**Step 6: Confirmation**
- You'll receive a confirmation email
- Booking appears in "My Bookings"
- Status: "Pending" until host confirms

**üí° Tips:**
- Bookings earn you 80 points
- You can cancel within cancellation policy
- Check cancellation policy before booking

---

### Managing Bookings

**Access:** Click "Bookings" in the navigation menu

**View Bookings:**
- **Upcoming:** Future bookings
- **Past:** Completed bookings
- **Cancelled:** Cancelled bookings

**Booking Actions:**

**1. View Details**
- Click on any booking card
- See full booking information
- View listing details

**2. Cancel Booking**
- Click "Cancel Booking" on pending/upcoming bookings
- Enter cancellation reason
- Review refund policy
- Confirm cancellation
- Refund processed to your wallet

**3. Write Review**
- After booking completion
- Click "Write Review"
- Rate 1-5 stars
- Write review text (up to 1000 characters)
- Submit review

**4. Request Refund**
- For cancelled bookings
- Contact host or use cancellation flow
- Refund percentage based on cancellation policy

### Points & Rewards

**Access:** Click "Points" in the navigation menu

**Earning Points:**
- **Sign-up bonus:** 150 points
- **Booking reward:** 80 points per booking
- Check your current balance

**Viewing Rewards:**
- Available rewards list
- Discount types (percentage or fixed amount)
- Minimum spend requirements
- Expiration dates

**Redeeming Rewards:**
1. Go to Points page
2. Browse available rewards
3. Click "Redeem" on desired reward
4. Reward is saved to your account

**Using Rewards:**
- During booking checkout
- Click "Apply Reward"
- Select from your redeemed rewards
- Discount applied automatically

**Reward Stacking:**
- Rewards can be combined with:
  - Promotional offers
  - Coupon codes
- Applied in order: Promo ‚Üí Coupon ‚Üí Reward

## Part 3: Host Guide

### Becoming a Host

**Step 1: Accept Host Policies**

**Step 2: Complete Host Setup**
- **Host Setup 1:** Basic information and Homes
- **Host Setup 2:** Experiences (if applicable)

**Step 3: Create Your First Listing**
- Follow listing creation guide (below)
- Publish your listing
- Start receiving bookings!

**üí° Note:** You can be both a Guest and Host simultaneously

### Creating Listings

**Access:** Go to Host Page ‚Üí Click "Listings" ‚Üí "Create New Listing"

**For Homes:**

**Step 1: Basic Information**
- Category: Homes
- Listing Type: (Apartment, House, etc.)
- Property Type
- Location (use map picker)
- Title
- Description

**Step 2: Details**
- Number of guests (adults, children, infants)
- Bedrooms
- Beds
- Bathrooms
- Amenities (add custom amenities)

**Step 3: Photos**
- Upload multiple photos
- Drag to reorder
- First photo is the main image
- Recommended: 5-10 high-quality photos

**Step 4: Pricing**
- Base price per night
- Cleaning fee (optional)
- Discount type (none, percentage, fixed)
- Discount value (if applicable)

**Step 5: Availability**
- Set available dates (start and end)
- Or leave open for all dates

**Step 6: Cancellation Policy**
- **Flexible:** Full refund 1 day before
- **Moderate:** Full refund 5 days before
- **Strict:** 50% refund up to 1 week before

**Step 7: Review & Publish**
- Review all information
- Save as draft or publish immediately
- Published listings appear in Explore

**For Experiences:**

**Step 1-3:** Similar to Homes (basic info, details, photos)

**Step 4: Schedule**
- Set available dates
- Set time slots
- Duration
- Maximum participants

**Step 5-7:** Pricing, policy, publish (same as Homes)

**For Services:**

**Step 1-3:** Similar to Homes

**Step 4: Service Details**
- Service type (Online/In-person)
- Duration
- Schedule availability
- Location (if in-person)

**Step 5-7:** Pricing, policy, publish

**üí° Tips:**
- Save as draft to complete later
- Use high-quality photos
- Write detailed descriptions
- Set competitive pricing
- Respond to messages quickly

**Managing Hosts:**

1. **View All Hosts**

2. **Filter Hosts**

   - By active/inactive status
   - Search by name or email

3. **View Host Details**
